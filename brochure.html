Nikoo Homes 9 Brochure

The Nikoo Homes 9 brochure gives you a consolidated overview of the project — floor plans, master plan, planned amenities, and pricing details — for this pre-launch residential development by Bhartiya City Group at KIADB, Bagalur, North Bangalore. Since the project is currently awaiting RERA approval, the brochure reflects the latest information available at the pre-launch stage and will be updated as further project details are officially released.

What's Inside the Brochure

The brochure is intended to give prospective buyers a single reference document covering:

  • Floor plans for the 2, 3 & 4 BHK configurations
  • Master plan and overall project layout
  • Planned amenities and open-space design
  • Location and connectivity details for KIADB, Bagalur
  • Indicative pricing available at the pre-launch stage

As the project has not yet received RERA approval, specifications, unit dimensions, and final pricing shown in the brochure are indicative and subject to change once the official launch documentation is released.
