The Nikoo Homes 9 amenities are planned to support modern urban living through a balanced combination of recreational, wellness, fitness, and community spaces. Spread across 17 acres in KIADB, Bagalur, Bangalore, the project is designed with approximately 80% open space, allowing residents to enjoy landscaped surroundings alongside everyday lifestyle facilities.

Rather than concentrating every facility in a single location, the overall planning is expected to integrate indoor and outdoor amenities throughout the residential community. As the project is currently in the pre-launch stage and RERA approval is awaited, the final amenity schedule and specifications will be released in the official project documentation.

Amenities Overview

Nikoo Homes 9 has been planned as a large residential development with approximately 1,500 apartments, where amenities are intended to complement daily living rather than simply increase the number of facilities.
