Floor Plans

Nikoo Homes 9 Floor Plan

The Nikoo Homes 9 floor plan offers carefully planned 2, 3, and 4 BHK luxury apartments designed to suit different family sizes and lifestyle requirements. Located in KIADB, Bagalur, North Bangalore, the project has been planned across 17 acres with approximately 1,500 apartments. The layouts focus on efficient space utilization, natural ventilation, comfortable circulation, and practical room arrangements for modern urban living.

As the project is currently in the pre-launch stage and RERA approval is awaited, the developer will release the detailed floor plans, apartment dimensions, and carpet area information during the official launch.

Floor Plan Overview

Nikoo Homes 9 has been planned to provide multiple apartment configurations, allowing buyers to choose a home based on their family size, lifestyle, and future requirements.

Floor Plan at a Glance

Floor Plan Element Specification
Project Type Luxury Apartments
Location KIADB, Bagalur, Bangalore
Total Land Area 17 Acres
Total Apartments Approximately 1,500 Units
Configurations 2, 3 & 4 BHK Apartments
Project Status Pre-Launch
RERA Status Approval Awaited
Possession December 2030 (Expected)

The detailed apartment layouts, super built-up area, carpet area, and room dimensions will be available after the official project launch.

Floor Plan Design Philosophy

The apartment layouts are expected to balance functionality with everyday comfort.

Instead of simply increasing apartment size, the planning focuses on creating efficient living spaces that support modern lifestyles.

The layouts are generally designed to provide:

  • Spacious living and dining areas
  • Well-planned kitchens
  • Comfortable bedrooms
  • Natural ventilation
  • Good daylight access
  • Functional circulation between rooms

This planning approach helps improve the overall usability of each apartment.

Apartment Configurations

Nikoo Homes 9 offers three apartment options designed for different household requirements.

2 BHK Floor Plan

The 2 BHK apartments are suitable for working professionals, young couples, and small families.

The layout is expected to include:

  • Entrance foyer
  • Living and dining space
  • Two bedrooms
  • Kitchen with utility area
  • Attached and common bathrooms
  • Balcony

The compact yet practical layout makes efficient use of available space while maintaining comfortable living areas.

3 BHK Floor Plan

The 3 BHK apartments are designed for growing families requiring additional living space.

Typical planning includes:

  • Spacious living and dining room
  • Three bedrooms
  • Multiple bathrooms
  • Kitchen with utility space
  • Balcony connected to living areas
  • Better separation between private and common spaces

This configuration offers flexibility for families requiring additional bedrooms or work-from-home arrangements.

4 BHK Floor Plan

The 4 BHK apartments represent the largest residential configuration within the project.

The larger layout generally provides:

  • Expansive living and dining area
  • Four bedrooms
  • Multiple bathrooms
  • Spacious kitchen
  • Utility area
  • Multiple balconies
  • Enhanced privacy between rooms

This configuration is suitable for larger families or buyers seeking additional living and work spaces.

A well-designed floor plan is not determined only by apartment size.

Efficient planning also considers:

  • Privacy
  • Storage opportunities
  • Natural light
  • Cross ventilation

Nikoo Homes 9 is expected to incorporate these practical planning principles throughout its apartment layouts.

Living Areas

The living and dining spaces form the central activity zone within each apartment.

These areas are generally planned to provide:

  • Comfortable family interaction
  • Flexible furniture arrangements
  • Good natural lighting
  • Easy balcony access
  • Convenient movement between rooms

A well-planned living area improves everyday comfort while creating a welcoming environment.

Kitchen Planning

Modern apartment layouts require kitchens that balance functionality with efficient movement.

The proposed layouts are expected to include:

  • Utility area
  • Storage provisions
  • Easy access to dining spaces
  • Proper ventilation

The final kitchen dimensions and specifications will be confirmed after the official launch.

Bedroom Design

Bedrooms are generally positioned to maximize privacy while maintaining comfortable access from the common living areas.

The planning typically focuses on:

  • Comfortable furniture layout
  • Natural ventilation
  • Daylight access
  • Wardrobe space
  • Convenient bathroom access

Well-positioned bedrooms contribute significantly to the overall usability of an apartment.

Balcony Planning

Balconies extend the indoor living space while improving natural ventilation and outdoor connectivity.

Depending on the apartment configuration, balconies may be connected to:

  • Living room
  • Master bedroom
  • Additional bedrooms

The final balcony layout will vary according to the selected apartment configuration.

Natural Light and Ventilation

Good residential planning places significant importance on daylight and airflow.

The Nikoo Homes 9 floor plans are expected to emphasize:

  • Cross ventilation
  • Window placement
  • Open views
  • Better daylight penetration
  • Comfortable indoor environment

These features improve both living comfort and long-term energy efficiency.
