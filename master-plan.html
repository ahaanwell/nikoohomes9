Master Plan

Nikoo Homes 9 Master Plan

The Nikoo Homes 9 master plan has been designed to create a balanced residential environment across 17 acres in KIADB, Bagalur, North Bangalore. The project comprises approximately 1,500 luxury apartments with thoughtfully planned residential spaces, landscaped open areas, internal roads, and lifestyle amenities. The overall layout focuses on improving everyday living through efficient planning rather than simply maximizing construction density.

With nearly 80% open space, the master plan aims to provide residents with ample greenery, walking areas, recreational spaces, and community zones while maintaining comfortable distances between residential buildings. As the project is currently in the pre-launch stage and RERA approval is awaited, the final master plan and detailed layout will be released with the official project documentation.

Master Plan Overview

Nikoo Homes 9 has been planned as a modern residential community that combines residential towers with landscaped outdoor spaces and shared lifestyle facilities.

Master Planning Philosophy

Instead of concentrating only on residential buildings, the Nikoo Homes 9 master plan gives equal importance to open areas, circulation, and community spaces.

The overall planning focuses on:

  • Efficient land utilization
  • Comfortable movement within the community
  • Large landscaped areas
  • Dedicated recreational spaces
  • Better privacy between residential buildings
  • Functional everyday living

This approach helps create a residential environment that supports long-term usability rather than only increasing apartment density.

Residential Layout

The project has been planned to accommodate approximately 1,500 apartments across the 17-acre site.

The residential zones are expected to include:

  • Well-positioned apartment towers
  • Internal access roads
  • Landscaped podiums and gardens
  • Open gathering spaces
  • Pedestrian-friendly circulation

The arrangement of buildings is intended to maximize natural light, ventilation, and open views wherever possible.

Open Space Planning

One of the notable aspects of the Nikoo Homes 9 master plan is its planned 80% open space.

Large open areas within residential communities improve the overall living environment by providing:

  • Landscaped gardens
  • Walking pathways
  • Green buffers
  • Children's play areas
  • Outdoor seating spaces
  • Community interaction zones

A higher proportion of open space also helps reduce the visual impact of high-density construction.

Internal Road Network

A well-designed internal circulation system improves daily movement throughout the community.

The proposed master plan is expected to include:

  • Clearly defined entry and exit points
  • Wide internal roads
  • Visitor movement areas
  • Dedicated pedestrian pathways
  • Service access routes

Separating pedestrian movement from vehicle circulation wherever possible contributes to improved safety and convenience.

Community Amenities Distribution

Instead of concentrating all facilities in one location, residential master plans generally distribute amenities across the community for easier access.

The Nikoo Homes 9 layout is expected to provide convenient access to recreational and lifestyle spaces from different residential blocks.

The final amenity locations will be confirmed in the official master plan released by the developer.

These landscaped spaces help improve the visual character of the development while providing residents with outdoor recreational opportunities.

Pedestrian Movement
